Whether you're a seasoned expert or new to 3D printing, we ensure clarity in our costing structure, making it straightforward and transparent for all our clients.

Smooth & Seamless Order Process

Ordering with Tunisia3Dprint is simple. Start by submitting your design. Our team then prepares the model for printing, ensuring its optimization. Once ready, we print and post-process your item, delivering it right to your doorstep within the Tunisian territory.

Maintaining Your 3D Prints

Your 3D printed items are built to last. However, like all things, they require care. To ensure longevity, avoid exposing prints to excessive heat and clean them with mild detergents.

With proper care, your 3D printed items can remain pristine and functional for years to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

Navigating the world of 3D printing can be daunting. Here, we've compiled the most common questions our clients ask about our 3D printing services, materials, pricing, and more. Get quick answers and dive deep into the specifics of Tunisia3Dprint's offerings.

How is the pricing for a 3D print determined?

Our pricing is determined by several factors including material type, print size, design complexity, and post-processing requirements. Additionally, discounts are available for specific models. We always strive to provide competitive and transparent pricing for our clients.

What materials can I choose from for my 3D print?

We offer a diverse range of materials tailored for different printing needs, from flexible to rigid, and transparent to opaque. Each material has its unique properties and applications. Our experts can guide you on the best material choice based on your project's requirements.

How long does it take from design submission to receiving the final product?

The duration varies based on the complexity of the design and our current workload. However, once your design is submitted and approved, we usually complete the printing within a few days. Delivery within the Tunisian territory may add additional time.

How do I care for and maintain my 3D printed items?

3D printed items are durable, but they require some care. Avoid exposing them to excessive heat and clean them with a mild detergent. With proper maintenance, they can remain functional and aesthetic for years.

Can I submit my own design for 3D printing, or do I need to use predefined models?

Absolutely! We encourage our clients to submit their unique designs. Our team reviews the design for printability and might suggest modifications if needed. You're not limited to predefined models; we strive to bring your vision to life.

What kind of technology and equipment do you use for 3D printing?

We utilize cutting-edge technology in our printing process, including advanced FDM, Resin 3D printers, and 3D scanners. Our commitment to technology ensures precision, quality, and efficiency in every project.
